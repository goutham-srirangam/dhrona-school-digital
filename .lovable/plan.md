

# A.S.R Dhrona School Website

## Overview
A modern, premium, responsive school website with 7 pages targeting parents. Features a red/blue/white color scheme, glassmorphism navbar, scroll animations, and emotional design.

## Pages & Key Sections

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero**: Full-width gradient banner with heading "Miryalaguda's First Future Ready School", subheading about holistic growth, and "Admissions Open" CTA button
- **Highlights**: Grid of rounded cards with icons (Yoga, Chess, Karra Saamu, Shlokas, Health Card, Lockers, Sports)
- **Why Choose Us**: Feature cards for NRI Management, Expert Faculty, IIT-JEE/NEET Foundation, MEP, MBM, MPG, Readers Win Rewards
- **Testimonials**: Parent/student quotes carousel
- **Gallery**: Image grid showcasing school life
- **CTA Banner**: "Admissions Open for Nursery to IX"

### 2. About Page
- Vision & Mission section with "Global Vision, Indian Roots"
- School philosophy: Physical + Mental + Cultural development
- Faculty highlights with cards

### 3. Academics Page
- Nursery to IX curriculum overview
- Concept-based learning approach
- Program cards: MEP, MPG, MBM with descriptions

### 4. Activities Page
- Activity cards: Yoga, Chess, Cultural learning, Sports, Indian Values
- Visual grid layout with descriptions

### 5. Facilities Page
- Feature cards: Health Tracking, Lockers, Transport, Safe Campus

### 6. Admissions Page
- Admission form (Name, Class, Phone, Email)
- "Apply Now" CTA with animated button

### 7. Contact Page
- Address, phone numbers, email display
- Google Maps embed placeholder
- Contact form

## Shared Components
- **Sticky Navbar**: Glassmorphism effect, school name/logo, mobile hamburger menu, links to all pages
- **Footer**: Contact info, quick links, social icons, "SMART way of Learning" tagline
- **Scroll animations**: Fade-in reveal on scroll for all sections

## Design System
- Colors: Red (#E53935), Blue (#1E3A8A), White, Light Grey
- Rounded cards with soft shadows
- Glassmorphism navbar (blur + semi-transparent background)
- Mobile-first responsive grid layouts
- Smooth hover effects and scroll-based reveal animations
